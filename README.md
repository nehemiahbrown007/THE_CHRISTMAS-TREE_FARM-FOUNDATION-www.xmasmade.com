Project Overview
The XMAS MADE platform is a circular-economy ecosystem designed to transform the Christmas tree industry from a seasonal waste-producer into a year-round environmental powerhouse. This repository manages the end-to-end lifecycle of the tree: from Farmer Support and 10-Year Subscriptions to Clean Blue Green Corp recycling logistics.

Core Modules
1. The Subscription Engine (/src/subscriptions)
The 10-Year Promise: A specialized billing and scheduling logic that allows users to pre-pay and secure tree delivery/pickup for a decade, stabilizing income for our farmers.

Logistics API: Real-time routing for tree delivery and "Last Day" pickups, ensuring trees are diverted from landfills to recycling hubs.

2. The Certification Portal (/src/certification)
The Evergreen Seal: An automated audit submission system for farms and corporations seeking the CTFF Verification.

The Registry: A public-facing database (JSON/GraphQL) tracking the impact scores of certified partners (e.g., Global Citizen Joe LLC).

3. The Digital Marketplace (/src/store)
Autonomous Product Delivery: Handles the 30+ digital assets (E-books, Carbon Calculators, Audio Soundscapes) that fund the foundation's operations.

Donation Tiers: Smart-contract-style logic that allocates a percentage of every sale directly to the Farmers’ Documentary Fund.

4. The "Clean Blue Green" Integration (/src/recycling)
Inventory Management: Tracks the conversion of recycled trees into household cleaners, wood chips, and fertilizers.

Impact Calculator: A front-end widget that converts "Trees Collected" into "Gallons of Cleaner Produced" and "Pounds of Carbon Offset."

Technical Stack
Frontend: Next.js (for SEO-optimized heritage storytelling)

Styling: Tailwind CSS (Forest Green & Arctic White design system)

Backend: Node.js with a PostgreSQL database (Farmer & Subscription Registry)

Integrations: * Stripe: For recurring 10-year subscription billing.

Mapbox: For the "Global Citizen Joe" World Impact Map.

Auth0: For secure farmer and corporate auditor logins.
